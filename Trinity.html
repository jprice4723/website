Trinity University

Trinity University is located in San Antonio, Texas. The university recently celebrated it's 150th year. Trinity is a small liberal arts school, but still offers tons of opportunities for its students.


Photo courtesy of Trinity University

Trinity has a beautiful campus located in the heart of San Antonio. There is no shortage of things to do on or off campus.

The features about Trinity that I appreciate most are the small class sizes, access to professors, and the beautiful campus.


I chose Trinity University because I felt I could get more out of it compared to a bigger school. Trinity has a unique combination of a small student body and a huge amount of resources.

I am thinking of majoring in accounting because Trinity has a very good accounting program with a 100% job placement rate. Here are some links to important information about my chosen interest:
